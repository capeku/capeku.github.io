Black Injustice

"Black Injustice" is a powerful painting that depicts the struggles and injustices faced by black people everywhere. The painting features a girl with one hand covering an eye, to represent how most black people in africa turn a blind eye to injustices, that affect other black people in the world. When people here Black lives matter they think, it's an American thing, this painting says its everyone's story, from injustices in Nigeria, Ghana, wars, exploitations etc. A close look at the hair of the lady shows the name of people who were victims of these injustices.
